A **blender** is an electric kitchen appliance used to mix, puree, or blend ingredients into smooth textures. It typically has:  

- **A motor** (to power the blades),  
- **Blades** (sharp, rotating parts that chop or blend food),  
- **A container** (usually made of glass or durable plastic),  
- **A lid** (to secure ingredients while blending).  

**Common uses**:  
- Making smoothies, shakes, or milkshakes.  
- Creating sauces, soups, or purees (e.g., baby food, hummus).  
- Chopping frozen fruits, vegetables, or ice.  

It’s a versatile tool for both cooking and food preparation! 🥄 blender 🍓
